Ethan McDonald (Fictional)

Ethan McDonald is a fictional representation of the author, Standy who appears as one of the protagonists of his book, "It Started With a Crash". He represents a fictional fork of Standy's life in the future where he isn't transgender.

Appearance

Ethan stands at around 5'11 with a weight of about 225 pounds.
