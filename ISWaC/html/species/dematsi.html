Dematsi

The dematsi are one of two bipedal intelligent lifeforms on Tupon.

Appearance

A dematsi's main feature is they have two eyes, unlike the tydgo. The shared features of both species are their antennaes.

Skin color

The dematsi can come in many colors. Pale, fair, tan, brown, ebony, obsidian, and probably more.
