Overview

The tydgo are one of two bipedal intelligent lifeforms on Tupon.

Appearance

A tydgo's main feature is they have three eyes, unlike the dematsi. The shared features of both species are their antennaes.

Skin color

The tydgo can come in many colors. Pale, fair, tan, brown, ebony, obsidian, and probably more.
