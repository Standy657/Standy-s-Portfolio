What is a "Kelwata Ledgi"?

The kelwata ledgi (Kehl-wah-tah led-ji) (Visceraflora monocula) is a omnivorous plant native to Akaria. Their 6 petals and their distinguishable eyes come in different colors. Petals may come in blood red, cherry red, orange, yellow, green, blue, purple, grey, brown, and possibly more undiscovered.
