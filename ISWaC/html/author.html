Ethan McDonald

Ethan McDonald (E-thin mick-DAWN-old), also known as Standy (stand-EE), is a graphic designer, coder, and writer. Around February-March of 2024, Standy began working on a book in progress known as, "It Started With a Crash". He got the inspiration from a Webtoon comic, "Down to Earth".
