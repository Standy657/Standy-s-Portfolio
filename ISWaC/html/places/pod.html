Appearance

Outside

A large spherical shape made of metal. Solar panels wrap around the pod.

Inside

There are three distinct rooms past the air lock. The "kitchen", the bedroom, and the bathroom.Inside the kitchen is a stool, a singular legged table, a simple stove, a central light, a large bowl, and 2 cabinets. The cabinets hold canned food and water respectively.

The bathroom just holds a port-a-potty like toilet.

Other

The sphere is a large gyroscope, keeping balance and gravity inside.

In order to survive in here, you would need to use some drinking water as hand-washing water. You wash your hands in the large bowl located in the kitchen and dump the water into space. Same with waste.
